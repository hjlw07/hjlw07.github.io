Information about Perfusionist

Perfusionists are specialized healthcare professionals who operate heart-lung machines and other devices to maintain blood circulation and breathing during surgery. They monitor blood conditions and support patients during organ transplants and critical procedures. Perfusionists also support in trauma care, chemotherapy, and congenital heart disease treatment.
